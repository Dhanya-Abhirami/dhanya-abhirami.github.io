Ignited Minds - A Book Summary

October 17, 2022 · 1 min

Problems in modern India​#

India has unlimited potential in terms of resources, education, business, energy.​ Yet we are in Self-destruction mode due to​

  • Faltering economy​
  • Rampaging poverty​
  • Dependence on foreign nations​
  • Disharmony among diverse groups

Key Takeaways​#

  • Youth rise to the occasion - learn, innovate and push the boundaries​
  • Wholesome education.​
  • Scientific temper and a spirit of enquiry​
  • Change in attitude( of limited achievement) and aptitude ​
  • Grab the opportunity or create the opportunity​
  • Patriotism beyond Politics and Religion​
  • Investments in Nuclear resources​
